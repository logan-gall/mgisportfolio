About

Welcome to this showcase of spatial data science projects! These projects come from Master of Geographic Information Science (MGIS) students enrolled in GIS 5572: Advanced Spatial Data Science class at the University of Minnesota. This portfolio is designed to be a showcase of the hard work, interdisciplinary interests, and cool projects that students are creating. These projects leverage tools like ArcGIS, Python, 3d modeling, and SQL to solve complex real-world challenges. Explore these projects and see how these wonderful students bridge the gap between theoretical spatial analysis and practical application!
